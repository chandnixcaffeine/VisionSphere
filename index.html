WORKFLOW

1

project requrements

The first step in our collaboration is submitting a project request. This helps us understand your needs and assess if we’re the right fit for your project. If everything aligns, we’ll schedule a video call to explore the details further.

2

Kickoff Meeting

Building trust is a key aspect of our collaboration. During our initial meeting, we’ll get to know each other and determine if we’re a good match. We’ll discuss your goals and requirements, address any questions, and outline the next steps.

3

proposal

Once we have thoroughly understood your project, we will craft a tailored proposal for you. Rather than charging by the hour, we work on a per-project basis, ensuring you have complete transparency and a clear understanding of the costs from the outset.

4

implementation

As soon as we have everything in place, we’ll begin the implementation process. Transparent communication and regular updates are our top priorities. We’ll keep you informed every step of the way through convenient channels like Slack.

5

launch

Before the launch, the finalized project will undergo comprehensive quality assurance to ensure every detail meets our high standards. Depending on the project type, we’ll assist with the launch
